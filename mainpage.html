It's Quiz Time!
Welcome to Quiz Time! The online quiz game testing your skills on JavaScript. You will be asked a total of 6 multiple choice questions. Upon starting, you will be given a total of 1 minute and 30 seconds to answer them all. For every incorrect answer, 7 seconds will be subtracted from your total time. Upon completion you will be prompted to enter your name into the High Scores if you score high enough.
